What We Do For You

From rent collection to legal support, LivingSimple Properties delivers full-service management with the proactive follow-through of an owner. Here is how we protect your property and your time.

Rent Collection

Tired of chasing tenants for rent payments each month? Go back to doing what you do best and leave it to me. Our digital portals make it easy for tenants to make payments online.

Maintenance

Say goodbye to long weekends spent resolving problems in your rental home. I process maintenance requests and coordinate personally with my (or your) vendors to get the work done right.

Screening

Problematic tenants cause endless headaches for property owners. I’ve developed an in-depth screening process to keep bad apples from poisoning your investment.

Legal Feedback

Struggling to understand the nuances of landlord-tenant law in MD/DC? … BEEN there, DONE that…. Rather than guide your way with a blind fold on through shark-infested water…. Get some direction from someone who’s seen it and has the resources to protect you.

Full Service

I might as well be an insurance company! Fully licensed and certified to BUY/SELL/LEASE, I want to provide my clients ability to handle any real estate situation. By offering a one-stop-shop, I can empower my homeowners with ALL their options upfront.

Responsive

Your investment is my priority. Therefore I make it a point we do business on a first-name basis. So you know exactly who is watching your property and what is going on with your investment.
