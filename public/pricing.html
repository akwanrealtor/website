Pricing FAQs

Answers to the most common questions from Montgomery County owners.

Do you charge onboarding or setup fees?

No. We only charge once we start managing rent-producing activity for your property.

How do leasing fees work?

Leasing fees are one month’s rent for new placements and include marketing, showings, screening, and lease execution.

Can I bundle maintenance projects?

Yes. Our Premium Protection and Investor Plus plans include project coordination for turnovers, renovations, and capital improvements.
