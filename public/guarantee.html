Our LivingSimple Guarantees

Every owner receives written protections that eliminate surprise costs and ensure you stay in control of your investment.

Schedule a Consultation See Pricing & Plans

Lease Guarantee

We place residents we would trust in our own homes—and back that promise in writing.

How It Works

If a resident we place leaves within nine months, we’ll market, screen, and place a new resident at no additional leasing fee. Our screening covers income, rental history, credit, and background checks.

Your Benefits

Minimize vacancy costs and maintain consistent cash flow. We manage the turnover process, coordinate vendors, and keep you updated at every step.

Eviction Protection

While rare, we are prepared. We cover the filing fees and manage the legal process.

We Handle the Process

If court action is required, we coordinate with our attorney partners, appear at proceedings, and keep you informed.

Cost Coverage

We pay the court filing fees up to $5,000 under qualifying plans. You stay protected from unexpected legal expenses.

No-Termination Fee

We earn your trust every month—no long-term lock-ins required.

Flexible Partnership

If our service does not meet your expectations, give 30 days’ notice and cancel without paying a penalty or termination fee.

Owner Confidence

We invest in relationships that last. Regular check-ins, transparent reporting, and responsive communication keep you informed.

Guarantee FAQs

More detail on how our protections support your investment.

Do the guarantees apply to every plan?

Yes. Lease Guarantee, Eviction Protection, and No-Termination Fee are included in every LivingSimple management plan.

How often do you need to use the Eviction Protection?

Evictions are rare thanks to our screening process. In the few cases when they occur, owners appreciate knowing the costs are covered.

What happens if I sell my property?

We support your transition. Provide 30 days’ notice, and we will coordinate with buyers, residents, and vendors to ensure a smooth hand-off.
