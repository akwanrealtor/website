Leasing Momentum: Keeping Your Rental Filled in 30 Days

A vacancy is the most expensive moment for a rental home. Our leasing process starts with high-quality visuals, data-backed pricing, and follow-up within the hour on every inquiry so your property stays in rotation. In 2024 we have averaged 19 days on market by pairing strong screening standards with proactive communication.

Atwan Kwan personally reviews each application, coordinates showings, and shares weekly status updates so you never wonder where things stand. Owners can log into their portal any time to see appointment feedback, pricing suggestions, and next steps.

March 20, 2024 · Owner Tips · by Atwan Kwan

Spring Maintenance Checklist for Montgomery County Owners

Spring rain and pollen can sneak up on HVAC systems, gutters, and landscaping. Our team schedules seasonal tune-ups with vetted vendors who know the Montgomery County and NW DC climate. We coordinate dryer vent cleaning, gutter clears, and exterior inspections to catch small issues before they surprise you mid-lease.

Download the checklist in your owner portal to see every task we tackle on your behalf—from testing sump pumps to refreshing mulch for curb appeal. If you prefer to use your own vendors, we will manage the scheduling and confirmation for a seamless experience.

What Residents Love About Our Benefit Package

Happy residents renew—and renewals protect your cash flow. This year we expanded the LivingSimple resident benefit package to include renters insurance compliance tracking, credit reporting, and a 24/7 repair hotline. The result: a 92% on-time payment rate and longer average lease terms.
