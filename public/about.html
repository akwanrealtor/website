About LivingSimple Properties

A Montgomery County property management team focused on high-trust relationships, proactive communication, and guarantees that protect your investment.

Schedule a Consultation Review Our Guarantees

From Family Investments to a Regional Team

Founder Atwan Kwan learned property care maintaining his family’s rentals in Rockville. Today, that hands-on approach guides a full team serving owners throughout Southern Montgomery County and NW DC.

We started by helping neighbors lease hard-to-fill homes and condos. Word spread quickly as we combined corporate-level marketing with concierge-style service. Owners wanted a partner who would pick up the phone, protect their investments, and provide transparent reporting. LivingSimple was built to answer that need.

Our portfolio has grown to include historic rowhomes, Metro-accessible condos, and luxury single-family residences. No matter the property type, owners receive a dedicated advisor, responsive communication, and our three guarantees—Lease Guarantee, Eviction Protection, and No-Termination Fee.

We operate like an extension of your team: coordinating maintenance, overseeing turnovers, and keeping you informed with proactive updates. That’s how we maintain a 4.9-star rating across Google and Zillow.
