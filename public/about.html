Hey There, I’m Atwan….

The LivingSimple Properties story starts with family investments and a lawn mower.

Atwan got his first taste of property management cutting yards at the budding age of 6. That job became important pretty quickly as the properties he cut were actually his family’s investments—helping pay for his BA degree at University of Maryland College Park.

Out of college, his first real job was a leasing agent for a large corporate luxury apartment property management group. Not too long, he leased his way to lead leasing agent in his building. And after learning from a multi-million dollar marketing budget, he helped friends and family lease and manage their properties with the same strategies and service.

After seeing how applying the same work generated more income for friends, he decided to go into business for himself! Soon enough he decided to get licensed and provide full management services of his own to clients…..

It’s now his passion and joy, helping private investors save TIME and MONEY in their investments…. just the same as saving for his old college fund from his family….
